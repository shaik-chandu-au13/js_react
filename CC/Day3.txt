Discount Calculation: Write a program that calculates the total price of items after applying a discount:

If the total is more than $500, apply a 10% discount.
If the total is between $300 and $500, apply a 5% discount.
Otherwise, no discount.


Tax Bracket Calculator: Implement a tax bracket system using if-else conditions:

Income ≤ $10,000: 0% tax
Income > $10,000 and ≤ $50,000: 10% tax on the amount over $10,000
Income > $50,000 and ≤ $100,000: 20% tax on the amount over $50,000
Income > $100,000: 30% tax on the amount over $100,000 Calculate and print the total tax based on the income entered by the user.


Password Strength Checker: Write a program that checks the strength of a password based on the following criteria:

Less than 6 characters: "Weak"
6-10 characters: "Moderate"
More than 10 characters, including at least one number, one uppercase letter, and one special character: "Strong" Use nested if-else conditions for this validation.
